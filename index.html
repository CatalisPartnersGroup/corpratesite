Our Technology

Real-Time PFAS Detection

Our proprietary organic nanoparticle method enables rapid, colorimetric detection of PFAS at concentrations below 100 ppt without the need for special equipment.

Scalable Production

Utilizing a unique reprecipitation process, we achieve precise nanoparticle size control, enabling mass production of high-performance detection reagents.
